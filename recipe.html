Brownie Recipe

Ingredient Amount Picture
Butter 1/2 cup
Sugar 1 cup
Cocoa powder 1/2 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla 1 tsp
Flour 1/4 cup
Salt 1/4 tsp
Chocolate chips 1/2 cup
Instructions
1. Pre-heat over to 350.
2. Melt butter in microwave, then add sugar and cocoa powder.
3. Add eggs and vanilla and whisk until shiny.
4. Stir in salt and flour.
5. Add in chocolate chips.
6. Bake for 20 minutes.
